The Brand

ABOUT TRIGGERFISH

Triggerfish is a biblical streetwear brand built around one idea: that the stories in scripture are not tame — they are raw, costly, and worth wearing on your chest.

Every design pulls from a specific passage. Lentils from 2 Samuel 23, where a man stood in a field and fought alone. Jawbone from Judges 15. Noah's MRAP from Genesis 6. These aren't decorations — they're declarations.

All pieces are printed on demand through Printful and shipped worldwide. Heavyweight cotton. Bold print. No compromises on quality.
